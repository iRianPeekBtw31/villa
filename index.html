Facilities

Luxurious Holiday Villa

This luxurious holiday villa comprises of two floors with three sun terraces. The ground floor is designed with an open-plan lounge, a separate fully equipped kitchen with a combined CD, radio and iPod dock, utility room (with washing machine and microwave), a double bedroom with air conditioning and a separate recently refurbished shower room with toilet.

Creature Comforts

The lounge includes a LCD satellite television giving access to numerous channels including all the terrestrial British channels and DVD player. This room also leads onto a large covered sun terrace, ideal for children and those who are not wanting to tan as they can feel the sun and be protected by the shade. This terrace is equipped with a table and six chairs ideal for those summer lunches and evening drinks.

Sleep or Sunbathe

The upstairs consists of two large double bedrooms both with air conditioning. There is a family bathroom with a second shower. The front bedroom is complemented with a petite sun terrace ideal for the romantics that looks onto pleasant views of the surrounding area. The second bedroom is equipped with a very large sun terrace ideal for sunbathing, complete with a second table and chairs and an airer for drying any washing.

Playa Flamenca

Playa Flamenca is approximately 45 minutes from Alicante and 20 minutes from Murcia airports, Transport to and from these airports can be arranged as well as private car hire facilities. The town is situated just off the N332 and the AP7 motorway, which links the major cities north and south including Alicante and Cartagena.

Local Facilities & Attractions

The Villa is located near a wide range of facilities and is an ideal location for families and golfers alike. Three 'Blue-Flag' beaches are located just a 10-15 minute walk, while numerous bars and restaurants can be found just 10 minutes away. A short drive are three spectacular golf courses. Another attraction is the 'Mar Menor' which is an island sea. This is on average ten degrees hotter than the rest of the Mediterranean and is famous for its therapeutic mud baths.

The nearest town is Torrevieja which is a 10 minute drive by car or taxi. This is a delightful Spanish town boasting a marina, waterfront market, numerous cafes, bars and plenty of street artists and entertainers. Near to the centre is a beautiful church square, while the superb palm lined promenade and harbour are ideal places for an evening stroll. Other attractions include a fun fair, go-karting and an aqua park.
